How to Perform Biceps Curls

  • Stand upright, holding dumbbells by your sides.
  • Keep your elbows close to your torso throughout.
  • Curl the dumbbells while contracting your biceps.
  • Pause briefly at the top of the curl.
  • Slowly lower back to the starting position.
